<h3>NSG Games Opening Ceremony - 21 January 2020</h3>
<p>Adelina Cheok, from Primary 6 Grace, was selected to represent Edgefield
Primary School to be the torch bearer for the National Sports Game Opening
Ceremony on 20 January 2020. Adelina had achieved first positions in all
the events that she had taken part in the National Rope Skipping Championship
2019.</p>

<h3>National Rope Skipping Championship 2019</h3>
<p>The pupils in Rope Skipping CCA participated in the National Rope Skipping
Championship 2019. They had won numerous awards in the championship. The
Junior Girls achieved overall first in the Junior Girls Division.</p>
