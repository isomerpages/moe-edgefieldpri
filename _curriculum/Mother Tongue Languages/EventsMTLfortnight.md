<h3><strong>The MTL Fortnight &amp; P4 Cultural Camp</strong></h3>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>It aims to create an immersive environment for
learning, encouraging students to use and experience their MTL in a fun
and engaging way. The fortnight encourages students to explore and experience
their cultural heritage through various activities, fostering a deeper
understanding of their identity.&nbsp;</p>
