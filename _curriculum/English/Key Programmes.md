<h2 style="text-align: left;"><span style="background-color: initial; font-size: 0.9375em;"><font color="#444444" face="arial, sans-serif">KidsREAD</font></span></h2>
This is a structured programme aimed at supporting  Primary 1 and 2 students with well-curated books and activities provided by NLB. The programme is implemented once a week after school for one hour.

<h2 style="text-align: left;"><span style="background-color: initial; font-size: 0.9375em;"><font color="#444444" face="arial, sans-serif">PVLEGS©</font></span></h2>
The following oracy programmes aim to develop every Edgefielder an empathetic communicator. Adopting Erik Palmer's Six Traits of Speaking©, Edgefielders familiarise themselves with Poise, Voice, Life, Eye contact, Gestures and Speed (PVLEGS©) as a guide to communicate effectively.

*   Primary 1: Show and Tell
*   Primary 2: Pick and Tell
*   Primary 3: Public Speaking
*   Primary 4: Persuasive Speaking
*   Primary 5 &amp; 6: Debate Programme
