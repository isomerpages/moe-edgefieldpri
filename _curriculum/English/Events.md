<h3>Language Week</h3>
<p>Language Weeks unfold with a vibrant array of activities, from captivating
storytelling sessions to stimulating debates and collaborative projects,
all aimed at fostering a love for English among our students. Through group
work and engagement with real-world issues, students discover the profound
impact of language in effecting positive change. This dynamic event celebrates
the beauty and diversity of English, equipping students with essential
communication skills and cultural awareness that <strong>spark a lifelong love of learning and connection through language.</strong>
</p>
