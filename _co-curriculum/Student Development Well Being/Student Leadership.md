<h3><strong>Department Goals and Objectives</strong></h3>
<p>Aligned with Edgefield Primary School's mission, the Student Leadership
Department aims to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Develop Positive Relationships:</strong> Empower students to influence
their peers positively through leadership roles.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Make Meaningful Contributions:</strong> Provide students with opportunities
to contribute actively to the school community.</p>
</li>
</ul>
