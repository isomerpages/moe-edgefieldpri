<h3>EDGE Approach</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Edge%20approachn.bmp">
</div>
<p>We believe that every Edgefielder is a leader and will create opportunities
for student leadership. Using the EDGE approach, our curriculum and programmes
will help students develop a deeper understanding about leadership knowledge
and skills.</p>
<h3>Experience</h3>
<p>The&nbsp;<strong>tiered-leadership pyramid</strong>&nbsp;is a spiral progression
that enables students to take on various leadership roles at the different
tiers.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Experience.png">
</div>
<h3>Develop</h3>
<p>All Edgefielders undergo the 7 habits of Highly Successful People programme
from Primary 1 to 6. This programme ensures that they are equipped with
the necessary skills to move towards self-leadership.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Develop.bmp">
</div>
<p>Tier 2 and Tier 3 leaders undergo&nbsp;<strong>MICEE</strong>&nbsp;trainings
and&nbsp;<strong>On-the-Job Trainings</strong>&nbsp;(OJT) to develop the
relevant skills to lead in their respective leadership roles.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/student%20leaders.png">
</div>
<h3>Grow</h3>
<p>Our student leaders receive feedback during dialogue sessions and regular
check-ins. Such opportunities enable them to be more reflective and work
towards having a growth mindset that will prepare them to be resilient
and adaptable leaders.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Grow%20(1).png">
</div>
<h3>Empower</h3>
<p>Student leaders are empowered to lead through various leadership opportunities
that allow them to&nbsp;<strong>M</strong>odel the way,&nbsp;<strong>I</strong>nspire
a shared vision,&nbsp;<strong>C</strong>hallenge current processes,&nbsp;<strong>E</strong>ncourage
the heart and&nbsp;<strong>E</strong>nable others to act (<strong>MICEE</strong>).</p>
